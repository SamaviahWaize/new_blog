BIRYANI BY KILO


Biryani by kilo offers traditional Hyderabadi and Lucknowi biryani cooked in individual handis delivered to your home. It has sent a benchmark among contemporaries because of its service,quality and taste. The menu is kept short and simple and even even vegetarians have few choices.They cook such phenomenal biryanis, very authentic, delivery is in a clay pot that just takes the taste a notch above. They have main course of Korma, kebabs and Nihari as well. The biryanis are of both kinds, pakki from Hyderabad and Lucknow and kacchi (raw) from Calcutta, just order any - all wonderful.

AMA CAFE


AMA Cafe, Majnu ka Tila, New Delhi is a fine cafe with beautiful interior and a warm ambience. It has one of the most soothing vicinities in majnu ka tila.Teas and coffee rule their beverage section. We would recommend one of their ice blended lattes to accompany your snack or sweet-dish. Alternatively, if you wish to ditch the sugar and go organic, they also offer fresh fruit juices.

XERO DEGREES


Xero degrees is such a delight to visit , this place has cozy seating with great soothing Bollywood songs and the ambience is just wow. Xero Degrees is famous throughout Delhi for its delicious fast food presented in a very cool manner. Have nicely cooked pizza, chicken wings and peri peri chicken at this restaurant when you happen to be near it. Great coffee will make your meal tastier and you'll surely come back.

THE PIZZA EXPRESS


The menu of this restaurant is recommended to Italian cuisine lovers. Trying perfectly cooked indian pizza, spaghetti and pizza salads is a pleasant experience here. At this place guests can delve deep into delicious dishes, and degust good chocolate cheesecakes, banoffee pie and gelato.If you are a pizza lover like me you must visit this place. They serve a huge variety of Pizza with delicious taste and expert quality. Their food is finger licking. Signature pizzas are must try. Staff is very polite and professional.
